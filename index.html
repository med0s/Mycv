About me

As a Hardware Design Engineer at R&D, I craft intricate PCBs for various applications, ranging from 12-layer designs to high-speed and high-density boards. I use my IPC-A-600 certification and over a decade of experience in electronics design and maintenance to ensure quality, reliability, and innovation in every project.

I am also the founder of a hardware design startup, where I apply my PCB expertise and passion for technology to create custom solutions for clients across different industries. I lead a team of talented engineers and collaborate with external partners to deliver high-performance products that meet the needs and expectations of our customers.

In addition to my professional endeavors, I am pursuing a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Thebes Academy, where I learn the latest trends and techniques in the field. I am also an instructor in embedded systems, Arduino, and hardware design, where I share my knowledge and skills with aspiring tech enthusiasts. I enjoy teaching and mentoring students, and I aim to inspire them to pursue their own goals and dreams in technology.
